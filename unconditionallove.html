JOHN BIGMUN



These hats started out as team uniforms for the "Walk With Us" fundraiser with the Lupus Research Alliance.

The team began with myself, my mother and my sister who lives with Lupus. One symptom of Lupus is acute sensitivity to the sun. Years back I bought my sister a bucket hat to make tough days in the sun more bearable. When it came time to come up with team uniforms for the walk, bucket hats made sense.With your purchase, I will donate all proceeds to the Lupus Research Alliance, the leading Lupus research organization and an organization that uses all of their proceeds to find a cure for Lupus through medical research.

Your support means the world to me, the Lupus for Research Alliance and the millions living with Lupus everyday.

Thank you for supporting and JOINING Team Unconditional Love.

With the utmost appreciation,
John of Team Unconditional Love
